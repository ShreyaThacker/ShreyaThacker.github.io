Portfolio

I am a seasoned healthcare professional with over ten years of experience in providing clinical care.

Throughout my career, my passion for improving treatment outcomes has been guided by evidence-based medicine.

Analyzing, evaluating, and documenting patient data over the years sparked my interest in data analysis.

To build on this foundation, I committed myself to gaining technical skills in Excel, SQL, and Tableau.

To showcase in this portfolio my ability to analyze complex datasets, draw meaningful insights, and present those insights as visual stories, I have completed multiple self-directed projects.

I am excited to bring my unique blend of clinical expertise and analytical skills to the field of data analytics!

Pizzeria Sales

This is a Microsoft Excel project that aims to conduct a thorough analysis of the sales of a pizzeria.

From data cleaning to analysis to visualization, this exercise utilizes and displays Microsoft's various powerful tools and features like lookup functions and pivot tables.

The analysis comes together with an interactive dashboard showing the seasonal sales and revenue trends, facilitating stakeholders' decision-making about the pizzeria's future commerce.

Montgomery County Automobile Crash

Montgomery crash reporting is an exercise in exploratory data analysis that intends to showcase a basic understanding of the process of data cleaning, data standardization, data querying, and data visualization using SQL and Tableau.

The project sets out to uncover trends like reasons for driver distraction, the effect of substance use on crash incidence, accident counts over time periods, and more.

The outcome of the analysis can aid in informed improvements in road safety initiatives.

Vegetable Supermarket

Vegetable Supermarket conducts a comprehensive sales analysis using SQL and Tableau for data querying and visualization.

The idea of this project is to provide actionable feedback by detecting purchase patterns and identifying key metrics like seasonal fluctuations, best-selling products, effect of promotional offers and such.

The user-friendly and intuitive tableau dashboard ties these observations into a visual story that could help enhance the store's performance.
